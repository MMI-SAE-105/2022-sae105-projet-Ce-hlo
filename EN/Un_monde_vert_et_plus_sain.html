Dicta

A cause to defend

Hope to me is like a life goal, something to fight for and hope for. I believe in a world that will suffer less in the future, a world where nature takes back its rights.

A futur and ecological actions

I hope that in the future we will be more ecological, it is a process that has been going on for a long time but if everyone changed only one small habit by making it greener it would still have an impact even if it is minimal. For example, when we let the water run so that it heats up, we collect it in a basin to water the plants so that the water we didn't use is still used for something and we don't waste it, of course everyone has his own way of acting for the environment.

Will technology be able to help us

We know that nature is going bad and that if we don't act quickly the consequences will be serious. So I hope that one day our technology will help to save the environment because for the moment yes we have evolved, yes we have made technological progress but all this does not help to preserve nature on the contrary. But there is not only that which counts it is also with us to act and to make our best so that that improves.

Reykjavik, the most ecological city

The most ecological city in the world is the capital of Iceland, Reykjavik. They use renewable energy for all the power they consume, their public transportation runs on hydrogen and their source of electricity is hydro-electricity and geothermal, so their standard of living is comfortable. But if we are aware of their ecological actions, I don't understand why we don't do the same, there is a ranking of the greenest cities, the most ecologically friendly, so why not do the same. Yes, we are improving, there has been progress and I hope that it will continue like this to evolve in a better world but also for the next generations.
