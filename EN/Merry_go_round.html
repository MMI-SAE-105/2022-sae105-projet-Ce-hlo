Dicta

Important music that makes me cry

In my opinion, it's important to talk about music because we know that music is an integral part of our life. There are many different kinds of music, from pop to electro to film music, of course I'm not going to present them all because they are numerous and varied.

Presentation of the music

The music I would like to talk about is a music from a Japanese animation movie, it is called Merry go round and it comes from the movie the walking castle. I chose this music because it moves me every time I listen to it as well as the movie that accompanies it. I know as soon as I listen to it that I will shed a tear because I am very sensitive to music and especially to the piano and there is a lot of it in the music

What it brings

I don't know if it's the same for you who read me but when I listen to music, I ask myself questions, I pay attention to the lyrics and I think to see if there are not lyrics that correspond to me. When I'm sad or lost I know that music makes me feel good and relieves me, it helps me to separate myself from my thoughts that I don't like or even to detach myself from them for a moment. You know when we are lost we listen to music and we forget our worries then we think of another person thinking as if we were in a movie. We can speak about feeling of escape, the music Merry go round speaks to us and listens to us experiencing these emotions, it tells us to let us go and that everything will be better one day.

Conclusion

So if you can't face your problems anymore, I think music is a good emotional support if you need to release yourself
