Dicta

A regret to improve

We all have regrets, they can be personal or they can be regrets about the society, the environment in which we live.

My regret in my generation

I have one regret, the behavior of people in my generation, you know all the categories and groups that we create to distinguish ourselves from people different from us or the judgment and opinions that we have on people even if we don't know them because we listen to the "they say that". Frankly it is something that saddens me to know that people who are said to be "different", "weird" are put aside because we base ourselves on an external judgment of the person when we don't really know him. Putting a person aside has an impact on him/her, most of the time mentally, he/she closes in on him/herself, wondering if he/she is the one who is different and has a problem when he/she is not the problem

The source of this judgment

I find that this problem is often found in elementary, middle and high schools, where there are groups of students who don't all know each other, or where groups of students will belittle and badmouth a person for no good reason because they don't know them.

Talking about it feels good

I talk about it because for me it's important, I find that the fact of writing allows to put down these ideas and to take away a heavy weight even if it won't go away entirely. In spite of that, we can succeed in building ourselves if we have the motivation, the desire and the conviction that we can do it. I think it's a shame that people are so judgmental and I hope that this will change in the future.
