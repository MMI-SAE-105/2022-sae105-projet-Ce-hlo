A book that made me feel good

Presenting a book? How does it help us in our personal development? Do we read in a mechanical way, I do not think we read because the book interests us, we take a particular interest in it, we take pleasure in it, it does us good and relaxes us.

What does reading do for us

I decided to introduce you to a book that is very close to my heart because I read it during the first confinement and it is thanks to this book that I started to read novels more and more. Before I read it I didn't think that reading could bring so much to a person. This book made me feel so many emotions, I enjoyed reading it so much that I didn't want to finish the book and keep on reading it again and again but in the end I don't regret it. I laughed, cried, was angry or even shocked while reading it. I hope that I will continue to read and that I will have what I wanted the most, a nice and big library where I can read and re-read the books I like.

Présentation of the book

This book I'm talking about is called "L'héritage d'Arachnée" by Marine Kellada. It is a fantasy novel with a love story, plot twists and a very advanced plot. The story revolves around a young woman who is going to meet a very mysterious man thanks to her father but this meeting is going to generate some changes in the life of the young woman who finds herself linked to this man. "Arachos, I promised that I would never leave you again, remember?"

What do remember?

I find that reading allows you to cut yourself off from the real world and to lock yourself in your bubble, it is a moment of detachment from reality. Literature comforts and reassures us thanks to these stories of all kinds. For me it's really like I change my universe when I read and it makes me feel better when I'm stressed or when I doubt.Even if you are not a reader I advise you to try it if you want to experience strong moments, emotions or escape from the reality that is yours.
